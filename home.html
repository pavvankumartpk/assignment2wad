Welcome to G-Shock Watches

About G-Shock

G-Shock is a brand of watches manufactured by Casio, known for their durability, shock resistance, and innovative design. Since their debut, G-Shock watches have become synonymous with toughness and resilience, attracting adventurers, athletes, and military personnel worldwide.

History of G-Shock

The G-Shock story began in 1981 when Casio engineer Kikuo Ibe sought to create a watch that would not break, even when dropped. After extensive research and development, the first G-Shock, the DW-5000C, was launched in 1983. This groundbreaking watch featured a unique triple-protection design for the case, components, and module, ensuring it could withstand shocks and impacts.

Over the decades, G-Shock has introduced numerous innovations, including solar-powered watches, atomic timekeeping, and Bluetooth connectivity, while maintaining its reputation for unparalleled toughness.

The Visionary: Kikuo Ibe

Kikuo Ibe, the visionary behind G-Shock, dedicated himself to creating a timepiece that could endure the harshest conditions. Inspired by a personal experience of breaking a precious watch given to him by his father, Ibe's determination and innovative spirit led to the development of the iconic G-Shock line. Today, his legacy lives on as G-Shock continues to set new standards in the watch industry.
